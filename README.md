# 🚆 UK Train Performance Analysis

**Reducing Delays, Optimizing Costs, Improving Customer Experience**

**Author:** Nguyễn Đăng Khôi – Data Analyst (Aspiring)

---

## 📌 Business Problem

The railway system is facing increasing **delays and cancellations**, leading to:

* Rising **refund costs**
* Declining **customer satisfaction**
* Inefficient **operational performance**

👉 Key question:
**How can we reduce operational failures while improving revenue and customer experience?**

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Reduce delay & cancellation rate: **15% → 5–10%**
* Cut avoidable refund costs: **~26% → 15%**
* Improve customer satisfaction through data-driven insights

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 1. 🚨 Core Issue = Operational Failures (Not Demand)

* **13% trips unsuccessful**
* > 50% caused by:

  * Signal Failure
  * Technical Issues
  * Staffing

👉 These are **controllable factors**

**→ Impact:** Directly drives delays AND refund costs

---

### 2. ⏰ Peak Hours Are the Bottleneck

* **41.9% of issues occur in the morning (6–9 AM)**
* Delay volume ~4x higher than other periods

👉 System overload during commuter hours

**→ Impact:** Small inefficiencies scale into major disruptions

---

### 3. 💸 Refund Costs Driven by Internal Issues

* **26.8% failed trips → refunds**
* Cost = **5.2% total revenue (£38K)**
* **86.6%** from operational failures

👉 Not external factors → **internal inefficiency is expensive**

---

### 4. 🛒 Clear Customer Segmentation

| Segment            | Behavior           | Driver |
| ------------------ | ------------------ | ------ |
| Price-sensitive    | Online, low fare   | Cost   |
| Convenience-driven | Station, high fare | Speed  |

👉 Two completely different decision logics

---

### 5. 🚆 Business Depends on Commuters

* ~93% trips = Short & Quick
* Peak: **6–9 AM, 4–7 PM**
* Short + Medium trips = **82% revenue**

👉 Entire system relies on **peak-hour stability**

---

## 🚀 Recommendations

### 🔧 1. Fix Operational Failures (Highest ROI)

* Preventive maintenance (signal, technical systems)
* Early warning monitoring at key stations

👉 Reduce both **delays & refund costs**

---

### 👥 2. Optimize Staffing for Peak Hours

* Increase staff during 6–9 AM
* Deploy flexible backup teams

👉 Remove system bottlenecks

---

### ⛅ 3. Proactive Weather Handling

* Integrate forecast into scheduling
* Notify customers early

👉 Reduce dissatisfaction (even if delays remain)

---

### 🎯 4. Leverage Customer Segmentation

* Commuters → season tickets
* Premium users → fast check-in & priority service
* Long trips → bundled add-ons

👉 Increase revenue without increasing operational cost

---

## 🧠 Key Takeaways

* The main issue is **operational inefficiency, not demand**
* **Peak-hour overload** is the critical failure point
* **Internal issues = direct financial loss** (refunds)
* Clear segmentation enables **targeted optimization**
